Approach

Our role-based training is the most effective training strategy followed by structured mentoring and coaching. It puts the training in the context of the role and what it takes to perform that role. This role specific approach results in a more efficient training process for trainees who learn the features, functions and transactions they need to know. The trainee will learn how to produce deliverables, follow the process, use tools and comply with the necessary reporting cycles and requirements.

This also allows trainees to gain a deeper knowledge of those features and functions through greater concentration on relevant, practical knowledge unlike traditional product-based training offered by most online training institutes which focus on an overall business process, without regard for the individual’s role.

A practical and problem-centered approach based on real examples is used in our training programs. The trainees will be given enough assignments/case studies during the learning process. These assignments give the trainees an opportunity to stretch past his or her current abilities. Role-based learning promotes positive self-esteem as well.

Placement Assistance

Our placement assistance is a six step process, which comprise of the following steps

1.Training & Assignments: All our Sr. Trainers use the Role Based Training approach to provide Online training. All Live or Video sessions are followed with Real-time project based Assignments.

2. Case Study & Real-time project Methodology: During the last week of the training, your trainer will work with you on several Case Studies based on a actual projects. This gives the participant an understanding of how things are accomplished in real-time environment.

3. Resume Preparation: We provide guidance in preparing an excellent resume because your resume is the first thing the client sees and first impression is everything.

4. Mock Interviews: With several rounds of mocks interviews, you get better at taking client interviews. The mock interviews are conducted in two phases – Technical phase and Project phase.

5. Marketing & Placement: Our consulting division will take over the marketing and placement process after successful completion of Mock interviews.

6. On the Job Support: After your successful placement at a Client site, our Senior consultants will guide and assist you for any project related questions.
